How to Use MCP Tokens
What is MCP?

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) allows AI assistants to directly interact with Solutionist on your behalf. With MCP, you can:

  • Ask AI assistants to read your solutions or search for specific solutions
  • Create new solutions or update existing ones through AI assistants
  • Get personalized solution recommendations while coding
Using Your Token with AI Assistants

When an AI assistant needs to access your Solutionist account, you can provide your MCP token. Here's a typical workflow:

  1. Tell your AI assistant that you want to use Solutionist integration
  2. When prompted, provide your token
  3. The assistant can now read and write solutions on your behalf
Security Note: Treat your MCP tokens like passwords. Never share them publicly. You can revoke tokens at any time if you suspect they've been compromised.
